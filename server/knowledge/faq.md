# SharpKode FAQ Consultant Handbook

## Pricing Questions
Q: How much does a website cost? A: It depends on whether the user needs a landing page, business website, corporate website, dynamic website, ecommerce store, or custom web app. Explain pages, design, content, SEO, admin panel, integrations, hosting, and maintenance as factors. Recommend the simplest package that achieves the goal.
Q: Why does custom software cost more than a website? A: Software includes workflows, users, permissions, database, reports, testing, security, and long-term support.
Q: Can I start small? A: Yes. Recommend MVP or phased delivery.
Q: Do you provide fixed packages? A: Packages can be used for standard needs, but custom projects need scope review.
Q: Is SEO included in website development? A: Basic SEO-friendly structure can be included; full SEO is an ongoing strategy.
Q: Do ads include media budget? A: Usually ad spend and management are separate; clarify during proposal.

## Timeline Questions
Q: How long does a website take? A: A simple landing page can be quick, while business, corporate, ecommerce, and custom websites take longer depending on content and features.
Q: How long does ERP take? A: ERP should be phased based on modules and user roles.
Q: Can you deliver urgently? A: Sometimes, if scope is controlled and content is ready.
Q: What delays projects? A: Unclear requirements, delayed content, changing scope, integrations, and approval cycles.

## Website Questions
Q: Can you redesign my old website? A: Yes. Review design, speed, SEO, content, conversion, and technology first.
Q: Do I need a website if I have Instagram? A: Yes, if the business needs credibility, Google visibility, structured information, and lead capture.
Q: Will my website work on mobile? A: It should be responsive and mobile-first.
Q: Can you add WhatsApp? A: Yes, WhatsApp CTA and lead tracking can be added.
Q: Can you add booking? A: Yes, depending on workflow.
Q: Can you maintain my website? A: Yes, maintenance can cover updates, backups, content, security, and fixes.

## SEO And Google Questions
Q: Can you rank my business on Google? A: SEO can improve ranking, but no ethical agency should guarantee exact rank. Explain local SEO, technical SEO, content, reviews, and competition.
Q: What is local SEO? A: Improving visibility for location-based searches and Google Maps.
Q: What is Google Business Profile optimization? A: Improving profile details, categories, services, photos, posts, reviews, and local relevance.
Q: How long does SEO take? A: SEO is gradual; local improvements can appear earlier, competitive keywords take longer.
Q: Should I run Google Ads or SEO? A: Ads are faster; SEO compounds. Often both work best.
Q: Why am I getting traffic but no leads? A: Possible weak landing page, poor offer, wrong keywords, slow site, weak CTA, or poor follow-up.

## Marketing Questions
Q: Do you manage Instagram? A: Yes, social media strategy, creatives, reels, content planning, and campaigns can be handled.
Q: Do reels help business? A: They help awareness and trust when tied to a content strategy.
Q: What is performance marketing? A: Campaigns optimized around measurable outcomes such as leads or sales.
Q: Do you create videos? A: Yes, content and video production can support branding and campaigns.
Q: Can marketing guarantee leads? A: Marketing can improve probability with strategy, tracking, and optimization, but market demand and offer quality matter.

## Software, ERP, CRM Questions
Q: Do I need CRM? A: If leads are missed, follow-up is manual, or sales status is unclear, CRM helps.
Q: Do I need ERP? A: If operations are fragmented across spreadsheets and teams, ERP may help.
Q: Can software integrate with my website? A: Yes, via APIs or forms depending on scope.
Q: Can you build admin panels? A: Yes.
Q: Can you migrate existing data? A: Often yes, after reviewing data quality.
Q: Is custom software secure? A: Security depends on authentication, access control, validation, hosting, backups, and maintenance.

## AI Questions
Q: Can you build an AI chatbot? A: Yes, for websites, support, lead qualification, and internal knowledge.
Q: Can AI replace my staff? A: AI should reduce repetitive work and support staff, not blindly replace human judgment.
Q: Can AI use my company data? A: Yes, if data is organized and permissions are clear.
Q: What is an AI agent? A: An AI system that can assist workflows, not just answer questions.
Q: Do AI systems need maintenance? A: Yes, prompts, knowledge, integrations, and performance should be improved over time.

## Hosting, Domain, Security, Support
Q: Do you provide hosting? A: Yes, hosting guidance and deployment support can be provided.
Q: Do you provide domain services? A: Domain guidance can be provided.
Q: Do you provide SSL? A: SSL should be part of a professional deployment.
Q: Do you provide backups? A: Maintenance plans can include backups.
Q: What if something breaks? A: Support terms depend on maintenance agreement.

## Consultation And Hiring
Q: Can I talk to the team? A: Yes. Provide phone and email or offer to collect project details.
Q: Do you provide free consultation? A: The team can discuss requirements and suggest the right path.
Q: What should I prepare before consultation? A: Business type, goal, current website/app if any, target audience, budget range, timeline, and examples.
Q: Can you sign NDA? A: If required, this can be discussed with the team.

## Objections
Q: It is too expensive. A: Acknowledge budget concerns and suggest phased scope, MVP, or priority features.
Q: I already have a website. A: Review whether it is generating leads, ranking, loading fast, and converting visitors.
Q: I need time. A: Encourage comparing options by long-term value, support, and business impact.
Q: I need approval. A: Offer a concise proposal with scope, phases, and expected outcomes.

## Additional Real Business Questions
Q: Should I build a website or start with social media? A: If customers search on Google or need trust before contacting you, a website is important. Social media is useful for awareness, but the website becomes the central proof and conversion point.
Q: Should I spend on ads before fixing my website? A: Usually no. Ads can bring traffic, but a weak page wastes budget. Improve landing page, offer, tracking, and follow-up first.
Q: Why am I not getting leads from my current website? A: Possible reasons include unclear offer, poor mobile experience, slow speed, weak CTA, thin content, no local SEO, poor trust signals, or no traffic strategy.
Q: Can you help if I already have a developer? A: SharpKode can support strategy, marketing, audit, UI/UX, SEO, or specific technical modules depending on need.
Q: What is the best first step for a new business? A: Define target audience and offer, then build a simple credible website or landing page with tracking and a lead channel.
Q: What should a dental clinic do first? A: Build trust pages, optimize Google Business Profile, collect reviews, create service pages, and make appointment contact easy.
Q: What should a real estate company do first? A: Create project landing pages, lead forms, WhatsApp flow, CRM, and ad tracking.
Q: What should a restaurant do first? A: Improve Google profile, menu visibility, reviews, photos, reels, and local search presence.
Q: Can SEO work without blogs? A: Sometimes, especially local SEO, but strong service pages and useful content improve results.
Q: Can I pause marketing after one month? A: You can, but marketing works better with testing and consistency. One month may only reveal early signals.
Q: Do I need CRM for a small team? A: If leads are being missed or follow-up depends on memory, even a simple CRM can help.
Q: Can AI answer all customer questions? A: AI can answer many repeated questions, but human handoff should remain for sensitive, complex, or high-value conversations.
Q: Is a mobile app necessary for my business? A: Only if users need frequent interaction, push notifications, booking, loyalty, delivery, or app-specific features. Otherwise a web app may be enough.
Q: What is conversion optimization? A: Improving pages, forms, CTAs, trust signals, speed, and messaging so more visitors become enquiries or customers.
Q: What is marketing automation? A: Using systems to trigger follow-ups, reminders, lead routing, email, WhatsApp, or CRM updates automatically.
