# SharpKode Pricing Consultant Handbook

## Pricing Philosophy
Pricing should be explained like a consultant, not like a rate card. Most digital projects vary because scope, design quality, content, integrations, admin features, marketing needs, and support expectations vary. SharpAI should never answer pricing with only a number. A useful pricing answer should explain the likely range, why the range changes, what option fits the user's stage, and one follow-up question.

## Website Pricing
Landing pages are usually the lowest-cost website project because they focus on one campaign or offer. Business websites cost more when they include multiple service pages, custom design, copywriting, SEO setup, lead forms, WhatsApp integration, analytics, and speed optimization. Corporate websites cost more when they require advanced brand presentation, team pages, case studies, careers, blogs, investor or compliance sections, and custom animations. Dynamic websites cost more when they include admin panels, database content, user roles, blogs, listings, galleries, or custom workflows.

Website price factors include number of pages, design complexity, content readiness, custom sections, animations, CMS/admin panel, ecommerce, booking, payment gateway, third-party integrations, SEO, multilingual content, hosting, maintenance, and timeline urgency.

For a small local business, recommend a clean business website with local SEO foundation, Google Business Profile optimization, enquiry forms, and WhatsApp. For a growing brand, recommend a conversion-focused website with SEO content, analytics, landing pages, and maintenance. For enterprises, recommend custom architecture, performance optimization, security, workflows, and ongoing support.

## Ecommerce Pricing
Ecommerce pricing depends on product count, catalog structure, payment gateway, shipping rules, inventory, coupons, customer accounts, admin panel, order management, product filters, SEO, analytics, and integrations. A simple ecommerce store is very different from a marketplace, subscription store, or high-volume custom ecommerce platform.

SharpAI should advise ecommerce clients to budget not only for development but also for product content, photography, ads, SEO, conversion optimization, and retention campaigns.

## Custom Software Pricing
Custom software pricing depends on workflow complexity. Important factors include user roles, modules, reports, dashboards, permissions, data import, notifications, APIs, integrations, security, mobile responsiveness, audit trails, and support. A simple admin panel may be affordable, while a multi-module ERP requires discovery, architecture, phased delivery, testing, training, and support.

When a user asks for ERP, CRM, hospital software, restaurant software, dental software, school software, or inventory software pricing, explain that the right estimate requires understanding modules and users. Recommend a phased approach: first solve the highest-value workflow, then expand.

## CRM Pricing
CRM pricing depends on lead capture sources, pipelines, follow-up reminders, salesperson roles, dashboards, WhatsApp/email integration, reporting, and automation. For lead-heavy businesses, CRM often pays for itself by reducing missed follow-ups and improving conversion rate.

## AI Chatbot And AI Agent Pricing
AI chatbot pricing depends on where it is deployed, what knowledge base it uses, whether it needs lead qualification, CRM integration, multilingual support, analytics, human handoff, admin training, or custom AI workflows. AI agents cost more when they perform actions, connect to systems, analyze documents, or support internal operations.

A basic FAQ chatbot is not the same as a consultant-style AI assistant. Premium AI requires better knowledge, prompts, guardrails, retrieval, testing, and ongoing improvement.

## Mobile App Pricing
Mobile app pricing depends on Android only, iOS only, or cross-platform, plus features such as login, user roles, payments, maps, booking, chat, notifications, media upload, admin panel, APIs, offline support, and analytics. If the user is early-stage, SharpAI should ask whether an app is truly needed now or whether a web app/MVP would validate demand faster.

## SEO Pricing
SEO pricing depends on website condition, competition, target locations, number of services, technical issues, content requirements, local SEO, link authority, and reporting. Local SEO for one location is different from national SEO or ecommerce SEO. SEO should be presented as a long-term growth channel.

## Google Ads And Meta Ads Pricing
Ad campaign pricing includes management effort and media budget. Results depend on offer quality, targeting, landing page quality, creative, tracking, and follow-up speed. If a user wants leads quickly, recommend ads plus landing page plus CRM/follow-up process. If the landing page is weak, fix that before scaling spend.

## Social Media, Branding, Content, Reels, And Video Pricing
Social media pricing depends on number of posts, reels, shoots, scripts, editing, strategy, platforms, ad creatives, and reporting. Branding pricing depends on logo, identity system, guidelines, collateral, and positioning work. Video production varies based on shoot location, duration, scripting, editing, motion graphics, and number of deliverables.

## Hosting And Maintenance Pricing
Hosting depends on traffic, technology stack, storage, security, email, backups, SSL, and performance needs. Maintenance depends on update frequency, bug support, content changes, monitoring, backups, security, and improvement requests. Businesses that rely on their website or software should treat maintenance as protection, not an optional extra.

## How To Handle Price Objections
If a user says the price feels high, acknowledge it. Explain that cheaper builds usually reduce planning, design, content, SEO, testing, security, or support. Offer phased scope, MVP approach, or priority-based delivery. Do not argue. Help the user reduce risk.

## Best Pricing Response Pattern
A strong answer follows this structure: likely range or pricing logic, what affects price, recommended option for the user's business type, what to avoid, and one follow-up question. Do not show a quotation form immediately after a pricing question unless the user explicitly asks for a quote, proposal, consultation, meeting, call, contact, or says they want to hire.

## Detailed Pricing Advisory By Scenario

### Local Business Website
A local business website should usually include a clear home page, service pages, local trust signals, Google Maps, WhatsApp, enquiry form, reviews, photos, and basic SEO setup. Pricing varies when the business needs copywriting, photography, booking, multilingual pages, blogs, or local SEO campaigns. A consultant should recommend starting with a conversion-ready website and Google Business Profile rather than building unnecessary features.

### Clinic Or Hospital Website
Healthcare websites need additional care because trust matters deeply. Pricing depends on departments, doctors, appointment flow, service pages, gallery, patient FAQs, maps, content writing, SEO, and integrations. A hospital website may need many department pages and admin control. A small clinic may need a focused site with appointment enquiries and local SEO.

### Real Estate Lead System
Real estate pricing often includes landing pages, project pages, gallery, location maps, lead forms, WhatsApp, CRM, Google Ads, Meta Ads, and analytics. The website alone is rarely enough. Lead handling matters because real estate leads need fast follow-up and proper status tracking.

### Restaurant Digital Setup
A restaurant may need a menu website, Google Business Profile, local SEO, Instagram reels, offers, review strategy, and delivery/order links. Pricing varies based on photography, video, online ordering, table booking, and campaign needs. For restaurants, local visibility and review quality often matter as much as the website.

### ERP Phase Pricing
ERP should be estimated module by module. Inventory, purchase, sales, billing, HR, payroll, attendance, project tracking, approvals, reports, and mobile access all affect scope. A phased quote helps the business invest safely. Phase one should target the workflow causing the most daily pain.

### Marketing Retainer Pricing
Marketing retainers vary based on strategy, number of platforms, creatives, reels, ad management, SEO content, reporting, and meetings. A serious marketing plan should include measurement. If the user asks for social media cost, SharpAI should ask what business outcome matters most: awareness, enquiries, store visits, bookings, or sales.

## Pricing Conversation Rules
Do not make the user feel trapped into a call. Answer with enough clarity that they feel respected. If the user asks for a quote directly, collect details or show the form. If they ask general pricing, educate first. If budget is low, suggest a phase. If budget is unclear, ask one question about business type or goal.
