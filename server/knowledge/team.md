# SharpKode Team Consultant Handbook

## Team Positioning
SharpKode's team combines development, design, digital marketing, AI, automation, and business consulting. Present the team as practical, collaborative, and focused on outcomes rather than only deliverables.

## Leadership
Sai Kumar is the Founder and CEO. Taj is the Lead Developer. When asked, answer clearly and briefly. Avoid inventing biographies, awards, employee counts, or certifications that are not in the knowledge base.

## Working Style
The team should be described as discovery-led, transparent, and focused on long-term partnerships. Good work begins with understanding the business problem, not immediately selling a package.

## Collaboration With Clients
Clients are expected to share goals, content, approvals, references, and feedback. SharpKode can guide planning, but successful projects improve when clients participate in discovery and review.

## Consultant Mindset
The team should recommend what fits the client's stage. A startup may need an MVP and go-to-market page. A local business may need local SEO before an app. An SME may need CRM before more ad spend. An enterprise may need phased ERP and integrations.

## Support Mindset
After launch, the team can support improvements, maintenance, marketing, SEO, ads, automation, and new modules. A good partnership continues beyond delivery.

## How The Team Should Be Represented In Chat
SharpAI should represent the team as thoughtful and execution-focused. The best tone is: the team will first understand the business, then recommend a practical path. Avoid exaggeration. Do not claim the team has worked with a specific client unless that client is in the portfolio context. Do not claim guaranteed rankings or guaranteed revenue.

## Long-Term Partnership
A client may start with a website and later need SEO, ads, CRM, automation, app development, or content. SharpKode should be positioned as a partner that can grow with the client. This is especially useful for SMEs that do not want to manage many disconnected vendors.
